How it works

Prompts base is designed to help you avoid the issues of creating prompts. As a hint, you can select the desired prompts through the categories. Choose a category > subcategory > click on the prompt you need. It will automatically be inserted into the line of your prompt. You can insert multiple hints from different categories at once.
